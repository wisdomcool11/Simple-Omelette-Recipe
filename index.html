Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  • Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the egg with a pinch of salt and Pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffer texture.
  • Heat the pan: Place a none-stick frying pan over a medium heat and add butter or oil.
  • Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubblin, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs is evenly coat the surface.
  • Add fillings (Optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edge but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your choosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  • Fold and Serve: As the omelette contiunes to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then serve it on a plate.
  • Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and Pepper if needed.
